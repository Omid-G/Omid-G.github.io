An electrical signal comes from the audio amplifier via the speaker wires and into the input of the transformer. The 220uF capacitor is used to help filter electrical noise. The transformer is used to step up the incoming AC voltage amplifying the signal. This is then applied across the master potentiometer which controls the AC audio voltage level across all three low medium and high potentiometers. The RC filters determine which frequency is allowed to pass though to activate the attached lights.
This project was made using hardboard from Home Depot as the display box, and a bandsaw to cut out the design. The 3D are provided below for dimensions and assembly instructions.
